About Me

Hi, I am Zhixu Li, a second-year Ph.D. student advised by Brilliant Prof. Jiachen Li at UC Riverside. Before that, I was advised by Prof. Qian Zhang. My current research interests focus on safety-oriented imitation learning (particularly diffusion policies) and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models. My long-term goal is to develop trustworthy embedded AI systems that provide safety guarantees for real-world applications.
